Car Contact is a database of licenseplates associated with phone numbers. Car owners may opt into the program so that anything occurs regarding their vehicle without their presence, they can be notified.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can people send me road rage messages?

- No. The system is designed so that only predermined messages with useful information can be sent.

Can someone use your system to find my phone number?

- No, Car Contact hides phone numbers from message senders. This way, you can communicate without revealing any of your private information to people you do not know.

What if someone sends me too many messages?

- Car Contact has a notification limit so users cannot spam other users within a certain period of time, or over the course of their membership.

What if I would no longer like to receive Car Contact Messages?

- If you would like to opt out of Car Contact, you may log into the website and turn notifications off for all license plates associated with your phone number.
